ABOUT US

DEEPLAND STUDIOS embarked on its journey in 2020 with a primary focus on the creation of game servers and the cultivation of vibrant online communities. Our initial efforts were centered around providing players with immersive and seamless multiplayer experiences, laying the groundwork for deeper engagement and interaction within virtual worlds.

As we honed our skills and expanded our capabilities, our vision evolved. In 2023, recognizing the potential to make a more significant impact on the gaming industry, we transitioned into a fully-fledged game development studio.

This pivotal shift marked the beginning of a new chapter for DEEPLAND STUDIOS, one where we are dedicated to conceptualizing, designing, and delivering innovative game projects that push the boundaries of creativity and immersion. With a passionate team of developers, designers, and others, we are committed to crafting unforgettable gaming experiences that captivate and inspire players around the globe. At DEEPLAND STUDIOS, we strive to create games that not only entertain but also leave a lasting impression on those who explore them.
